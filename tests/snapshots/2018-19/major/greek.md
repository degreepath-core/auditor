# Greek
For this major, you must complete “Language”, “Classics”, and “Ancient Greek History”.

## Language
For this requirement, you must take seven of the following courses:

- GREEK 112
- GREEK 231
- GREEK 253
- GREEK 370
- GREEK 372

- GREEK 375
